title:  The Martha
image:  /images/martha.jpg
description: |-
  We all know the venerable Gin & Juice, but did you know that it has a
  smoking buddy who doesn't quite go so hard? Or, well.... at least she
  doesn't _tell you_ about it. This cocktail is a bit reserved, but really
  lets the gin shine through. Have fun and try different juices with it!

  And should you be inclined to respect tradition, you can always enjoy
  it with a blunt behind the building.

ingredients:
  - 2 parts cold smoked Dissent gin
  - 1/2 part pineapple juice
  - 1 part tonic
  - orange twist

---
Add the liquids into a mixing glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with an orange twist.
