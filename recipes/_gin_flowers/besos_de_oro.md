title:  Besos de Oro
image:  /images/golden_kiss.jpg
description: |-
  The "golden kiss" brings several strong flavors together into one harmonious
  whole. The Gracias a Dios gin starts with a mezcal base so it's smooth, sultry,
  and smoky. Nixta, on the other hand, is sweet warm honey cornbread melting in
  your mouth.

  Maybe you'd like to share a golden kiss with someone special?

ingredients:
  - 1 part Gracias a Dios Agave Gin Oaxaca
  - 1 part Nixta Licor de Elote
  - 1/2 part sweet vermouth
  - a dash or two of chocolate bitters to taste
  - lime wedge

---
Add the liquids into a mixing glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a lime wedge. Squeeze in a
few drops if you'd like.
