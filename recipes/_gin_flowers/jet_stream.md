title:  Pink Jet Stream
image:  /images/jet_stream_amelia.jpg
description: |-
  Amelia Earhart was the first woman to fly solo nonstop across the Atlantic
  Ocean in 1932. Just five years later, she disappeared while circumnavigating
  the globe. We weren't ready for her level of badassery.

  She left behind a legacy that inspired countless women, men, and every other
  identity. Today we honor her memory with a cocktail that's light and refreshing,
  while still being full of vibrant flavors, just like Amelia herself.

  You can enjoy it booze-free with the Pink London Spirit, or try the Empress
  Gin. And for a fun little boost, if you get the ratio just right the deep
  purple of Empress will color shift to pink!

ingredients:
  - 1 1/2 parts Pink London Spirit (or Empress Gin)
  - 1 part lime juice
  - 1 part simple syrup
  - 1 part tonic water
  - lime wedge

---
Add the liquids into a mixing glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a lime wedge.
